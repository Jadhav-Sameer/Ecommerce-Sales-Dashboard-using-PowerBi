# Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

## Overview

The Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product trends, and payment preferences. The dashboard provides actionable insights that help businesses monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify top-performing products and regions, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and profitability.

## Business Objective

To develop a comprehensive sales analytics dashboard that enables stakeholders to:

* Track overall sales performance and profitability.
* Identify top-performing states and customers.
* Analyze product category and sub-category performance.
* Understand customer purchasing patterns.
* Monitor sales trends across different quarters.
* Evaluate preferred payment methods.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales Amount:** 161K
* **Total Profit:** 26K

## Key Insights

* Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh contribute significantly to total sales.
* Clothing category accounts for the highest quantity sold.
* Printers and Phones generate the highest profit among sub-categories.
* Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most preferred payment method.
* January recorded the highest monthly profit compared to February and March.
* A small group of customers contributes a significant share of overall revenue.
